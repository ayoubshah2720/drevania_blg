Questions

  • Should I provide the scheduled route beforehand?

    Providing the route and stops before the trip helps chauffeurs plan better, save time, and steer clear of traffic. It also lets Drivania’s global dispatchers keep an eye on the trip schedule to ensure everything runs on time.

  • Can I change or extend my trip with the driver, or do I need to contact Drivania's customer support?

    While you can inform the driver first, it’s important to coordinate any trip changes or extensions with Drivania’s customer support. This ensures all your plans are updated in a coordinated manner. Our dedicated team is here to assist you every step of the way.

  • Can I keep the same driver for a multi-day trip?

    We strive to ensure you have the same driver throughout your trip, unless conflicts with their schedule or unforeseen personal events arise.

  • Does my chauffeur wait if my flight is delayed?

    Our customer support team and your chauffeur actively track your flight status in real time, adjusting the pickup time as needed. Rest assured that your driver will wait for you, even if your flight is delayed.

  • When do I receive the driver’s contact details?

    For Drivania’s top 200 destinations, you’ll get your chauffeur’s details 4 to 7 days before pickup. For other destinations, we’ll send them at least 24 hours before your pickup, giving you time for any adjustments or special requests.

  • Do vehicles bring on-board data connectivity?

    While certain vehicles come equipped with WiFi and Bluetooth connectivity, for reliable and immediate internet access in most locations, we suggest obtaining a local SIM card or setting up an e-SIM before you leave.
