Claudionor’s support during a roadshow in São Paulo

I have always been an entrepreneur, and as a such, I have often traveled around the world to showcase my compa ny’s latest digital products. I like to travel efficiently, and I remember one particular time when I had to go to São Paulo for a roadshow. My schedule was full for the three days I was in the city, with meetings spread across different neighborhoods.

Knowing I needed to manage my time in a very efficient manner, I booked chauffeured transportation through my travel agency. Claudionor met me at the airport in an impeccably maintained car. He was very well-mannered and groomed, features I highly appreciate when I am traveling for business.

Claudionor’s expertise was truly invaluable. He mapped out the most efficient routes each day, avoiding traffic hotspots and ensuring that I arrived on time for every presentation. On top of that, before each meeting, he reached out to venues ahead of time, securing convenient parking and checking entry points so that I could walk straight in without delay. This attention to detail allowed me to focus entirely on the business at hand without worrying about logistics.

One particularly memorable moment was when a last-minute meeting with a prospect popped up on the opposite side of the city. Claudionor swiftly adjusted the schedule, finding a quiet café en route where I could take a quick call to confirm details. His ability to adapt and anticipate my needs throughout the roadshow meant I maximized my time in São Paulo, turning a business trip into a productive and efficient experience.

“When business travel gets busy, staying ahead of the game is crucial. I take care of the details so that my clients can focus on making their time in the city a success.”

    Brazilian Court São Paulo

  2. 02  

    Baretto

    A refined bar within the Fasano Hotel, offering live music performances in a sophisticated setting. Address: Rua Vittorio Fasano, 88 - Jardins, São Paulo, SP.

  3. 03  

    Dalva e Dito

    A restaurant by Chef Alex Atala that serves sophisticated Brazilian dishes in a cozy, welcoming environment. Address: R. Padre João Manuel, 1115 - Jardins, São Paulo, SP.

  4. 04  

    Sala São Paulo

    A majestic concert hall home to the São Paulo State Symphony, housed in a beautifully renovated railway station. Address: Praça Júlio Prestes, 16 - Campos Elíseos, São Paulo, SP.

  5. 05  

    Tivoli Mofarrej São Paulo Hotel

    Known for its luxurious services, this hotel also hosts the upscale Anantara Spa. Address: Alameda Santos, 1437 - Jardim Paulista, São Paulo, SP.

  6. 06

    A luxury hotel offering exquisite suites and a high level of privacy and comfort. Address: Alameda Casa Branca, 909 - Jardins, São Paulo, SP.

  7. 07  

    Maní

    A Michelin-starred restaurant blending Brazilian ingredients with contemporary culinary techniques. Address: Rua Joaquim Antunes, 210 - Pinheiros, São Paulo, SP.

  8. 08  

    Oscar Freire Street

    São Paulo’s most luxurious shopping street, lined with designer boutiques, exclusive jewelry stores, and chic cafes.

  9. 09  

    Pobre Juan

    High-end Argentine steakhouse known for its prime cuts of meat and extensive wine list. Address: Av. Magalhães de Castro, 12000 - Morumbi, São Paulo, SP.

Claudionor's recommended hotel: Tivoli Mofarrej São Paulo Hotel

Tivoli Mofarrej São Paulo Hotel combines comfort and style, making it an excellent choice for those visiting the heart of São Paulo. Located in the lively Jardins neighborhood, the hotel is within easy reach of the city’s best shopping streets, cultural attractions, and a variety of restaurants. The interiors are designed with a contemporary touch, offering a warm and welcoming atmosphere for both business and leisure guests. The hotel also provides several facilities, including a relaxing spa, an outdoor pool, and a rooftop restaurant with stunning views of the city.

The rooms and suites at Tivoli Mofarrej are thoughtfully designed to be both comfortable and stylish, with modern amenities and plenty of space for guests to unwind after a day exploring São Paulo. Guests can also enjoy personalized services, from in-room dining to concierge support, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable stay. The hotel’s attentive team is always ready to offer recommendations and help make each visit special. Address: Alameda Santos, 1437 – Jardins, São Paulo – SP, 01419-905, Brazil.
